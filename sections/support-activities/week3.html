Checking your finished pen

Once you have assembled your pen, take a moment to check that it functions and looks good. Test that the refill extends and retracts smoothly and that the pen writes. Examine the joint between the wood and the metal components: there should be no gaps or uneven surfaces. The finish should feel smooth to the touch and the pen should be comfortable to hold.

Reflecting for next time

Thinking about what went well and what was difficult helps you improve. Were you happy with the shape you turned? Did you sand enough to get a smooth finish? Did the glue have time to dry? Be honest about mistakes so you can avoid them next time. Use the space in your journal or portfolio to record your thoughts and any changes you would make for your next project.
