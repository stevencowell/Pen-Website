Assessment

Your pen project will be assessed on the following criteria:

  • Safe workshop practices: Consistently follow WHS procedures, use tools correctly and maintain a tidy workspace.
  • Quality of production: Accuracy in measuring, drilling and turning, smooth finish and neat assembly with no gaps or defects.
  • Completion of theory work: Submit quiz results through this website and participate in class activities and discussions.
  • Reflection and evaluation: Provide thoughtful responses in your evaluation, identifying successes, challenges and areas for improvement.

Make sure to complete each quiz and reflection by the due dates. Your teacher will use the online spreadsheet to record results.
