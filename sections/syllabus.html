The Pen Making project addresses multiple outcomes from the Stage 5 Industrial Technology syllabus. Through this unit you will:

  • IND56 – Demonstrate safe workshop practices and risk management: You will learn and apply Work Health and Safety (WHS) procedures when operating the lathe and other tools, and identify hazards and mitigation strategies.
  • IND57 – Apply design and production skills: By turning and assembling a pen you will develop hands‑on skills in measuring, cutting, drilling, turning, sanding, finishing and assembling components.
  • IND58 – Evaluate products: In the final week you will evaluate your pen’s functional and aesthetic qualities, consider environmental and economic factors and reflect on improvements.

Throughout the unit you will also practise communication skills through your folio and quizzes, and transfer your knowledge to new contexts.
