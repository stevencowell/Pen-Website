Pen Making Project

In this unit you will design and produce a wooden pen using a commercial pen kit and a wood lathe. The project combines creative design with practical skills and provides an opportunity to explore the properties of timber and the mechanics of turning.

During the course you will:

  • Select and prepare timber blanks for turning.
  • Learn how to mount blanks on a mandrel and use a lathe safely to shape the pen barrels.
  • Sand and finish the wood to a smooth, attractive surface.
  • Assemble the pen components using the provided kit.
  • Evaluate your finished pen and reflect on your learning.

The project encourages creativity and craftsmanship while reinforcing safety and risk management in the workshop. Use the resources and activities throughout this website to support your learning.
